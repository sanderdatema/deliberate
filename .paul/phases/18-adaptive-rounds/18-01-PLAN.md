<objective>
## Goal
Replace fixed `preset.rounds` with adaptive round control: a ConvergenceAgent (functional, no Persona) evaluates after each analyst round whether continuing adds value. The preset `rounds` field becomes `max_rounds` (upper bound), and a new `min_rounds` field (default 1) sets the floor. The convergence agent decides whether to continue or stop between min and max.

## Purpose
Fixed round counts waste tokens when analysts converge early (round 2 adds nothing new) or stop too soon when positions are still diverging. Adaptive rounds make deliberation smarter and more cost-efficient.

## Output
- `ConvergenceResult` dataclass (should_continue, reason, round_number)
- `_run_convergence_check()` using `_call_functional_agent()` (Phase 17 pattern)
- `_CONVERGENCE_SYSTEM_PROMPT` in engine.py
- `_parse_convergence_output()` static method
- Engine `run()` loop: min_rounds guaranteed, then convergence check each round until max_rounds
- New events: `convergence_started`, `convergence_completed`
- Preset model updated: `rounds` → `max_rounds`, `min_rounds` added
- config.yaml updated with min_rounds/max_rounds per preset
</objective>
